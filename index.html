About Us

At PMB Curtainwalling Ltd, we specialise in delivering high-quality curtain wall and façade installation services throughout the UK. With extensive industry experience and a dedicated team of experts, we provide complete solutions for all types of exterior building envelopes — from cutting-edge glass curtain walls to premium cladding systems.

 We take pride in working closely with main contractors, developers, and architects to ensure every project meets the highest standards of safety, performance, and architectural excellence. Our reputation is built on reliability, precision, and a commitment to delivering projects on time and within budget.

 Whether it’s a large-scale commercial development or an exclusive residential project, you can rely on us to manage every detail of the façade installation with care and craftsmanship. From initial design coordination to final handover, we make sure your building stands out for all the right reasons.

Our Services

PMB Curtain Walling Ltd is a London-based, family-owned business with more than 15 years of industry experience, offering fully integrated façade and curtain walling services. We pride ourselves on completing every project to exacting standards, prioritising safety, efficiency, and long-term performance to consistently exceed client expectations.

Curtain Wall Installation

Expert installation of unitised and stick curtain wall systems for commercial and residential buildings.

Rainscreen Cladding

Modern rainscreen cladding systems that enhance building performance and appearance.

Aluminium and Glass Façades

Bespoke aluminium and glass façades, designed and installed for maximum durability and visual impact.

Louvres and Solar Shading

Functional and aesthetic louvre systems and sun shading solutions to improve energy efficiency and occupant comfort.

Façade Maintenance and Refurbishment

Inspection, repair and refurbishment services to maintain façade integrity and prolong lifespan.

Design Coordination and Consultation

Technical support and design input to ensure seamless integration with the building structure and compliance with all standards.

No matter the complexity, our dedicated team is ready to deliver a façade solution that exceeds expectations.
